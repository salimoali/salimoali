## What I work on

I'm at the intersection of engineering and systems thinking — focused on how industrial systems can transition toward sustainability. My work spans energy modelling, circular economy analysis, and life cycle assessment.

- 🔋 **Energy systems** — stochastic demand modelling (RAMP), energy optimisation (Calliope), renewable transition planning
- ♻️ **Circular economy** — strategy assessment, viability analysis, sustainability-linked finance
- 🌍 **Industrial ecology** — LCA, material flow analysis, sociotechnical transitions

Before the MSc I worked in **gas turbine efficiency** — which is where my interest in system-level optimisation started. That has since expanded: from component efficiency to institutional mechanisms, policy design, and what actually makes sustainability transitions stick.
